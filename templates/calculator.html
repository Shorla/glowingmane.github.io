Tips for hair measurement

Tools needed

  1. Flexible measuring tape or Ruler
  2. A mirror
  3. A pen or marker

To measure hair growth, you can follow these steps:.

  • Choose a Starting Point: Decide where you want to measure your hair growth. Common areas include the scalp or specific sections of hair.
  • Part Your Hair: Use a comb or your fingers to part your hair, making sure you can clearly see the area you want to measure.
  • Measure Length: Carefully place the measuring tape or ruler at the base of the hair shaft (near the scalp) and gently extend it along the length of the hair. Ensure you follow the natural curve of the hair, especially if it's curly.
  • Record Measurements: Use the pen or marker to mark the length of the hair on the tape or ruler. You can also take a photo for reference.
  • Monitor Progress: Repeat the measurement periodically (e.g., every month) to track your hair growth progress. Input the value in the box below to store your values for the first four months.
